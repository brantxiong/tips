** start with gnome **
create and edit the file "~/.config/autostart/fcitx.desktop", add the following lines
[Desktop Entry]
Type=Application
Exec=/usr/bin/fcitx
Hidden=false
NoDisplay=false
X-GNOME-Autostart-enabled=true
Name[zh_CN]=fcitx
Name=fcitx
Comment[zh_CN]=
Comment=

the "Exec" field depends on your fcitx installation, and here is default for "apt-get install"
